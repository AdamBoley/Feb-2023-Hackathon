Have you ever found it difficult to find an NGO of your liking to contribute to? Maybe you're working on a presentation to pitch a potential cause or NGO to your collegues and bosses at work? Or maybe you're just like us and find it tiring to scan the web for the perfect NGO whenever you're feeling generous?

Then look no further! We are here just for you.

Our goal here at Friends of Earth is to make it easier for everyone to contribute towards a better world. Whether you already have a cause that you are passionate about, or want to find a cause to be passionate about, we want to provide you with a structured overview of the options you have to make this world of ours a better place for all!
